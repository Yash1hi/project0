  • Pokemon have different effectiveness on other depending on the move they use and the other pokemon
  • If a pokemon uses a move that is the same type as itself, it will gain a small damage bonus
  • The type of the move that is used is checked against the opposing pokemon's type to check effectiveness
  • Two type pokemon will have different effects
    1. A pokemon with two types will have the modfier take priority
    2. A pokemon with two types that are both weak to one type will take x4 damage from that type of move
    3. A pokemon with two types that both take less damage from a move will take x0.25 damage from that type of move
    4. A pokemon with one type that is weak and one that takes less damage will take normal damage from that move
